About us

Who we are

AcePlus is a social enterprise, committed to capacity building, simplifying business processes and the growth of startups and small businesses in Nigeria. Our product offerings have been tailored to enable entrepreneurs hit the ground running with their ideas and existing businesses to grow as well as run their day-to-day business more efficiently, saving thousands – perhaps millions – that would hitherto been spent as fees on consultants, lawyers and accountants. They include professional-grade ready-to-print templates, forms and guides, saving hundreds of hours of writing and formatting documents.

Our products

Our flagship product Business Toolkit is designed to enable startups and small businesses launch, run and grow. We understand startups and small business because we are one. We know what it’s like to start from zero, with no outside investment, and build a company from one employee to many. Our products are tooled for small businesses because we understand the challenges of startups, and we also know what it takes to be successful. We are committed to providing that knowledge, experience, and expertise to as many other small businesses as possible.

Our plan

Our Free Plan is always free – no trial period. Only pay for more features when you’re ready. No contracts or hidden fees, ever. Our Starter Plan enables entrepreneurs develop their business plan in very little time, enabling them to seek investments or loans. It comes with tools that help them work through their financials – even for those that are not math savvy. Should they require hands-on support, the Advanced Plan offers a Customized Business Plan by our dedicated team of writers, expert advice, as well as over 30 free business e-books and videos from the best minds across the globe relevant to starting and growing businesses. Should these not be enough and one needs more with anything from Ideation and Strategy Development to Fit-for-Purpose Growth Strategy, our Professional Plan would come in handy. In this case, do get in touch for a more customized plan.
